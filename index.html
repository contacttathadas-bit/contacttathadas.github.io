MG Eyewear: International Luxury

Visual Designer & Art Director — morrisgarageseyewear.com

The Challenge

MG Eyewear needed a complete visual overhaul on their social channels. The challenge was to create high-end, premium posts that seamlessly matched the aesthetic standards of top-tier international luxury brands.

The Approach

I abandoned generic product templates and focused on cinematic lighting, sharp typography, and minimalist compositions. By treating each social media post like a page in a high-fashion editorial magazine, we created a cohesive, highly desirable feed.

The Impact

The new aesthetic established immediate visual trust, positioning MG Eyewear alongside global competitors and significantly increasing engagement from high-intent demographics.
