Hyper-Realistic Product Identity

Visual Director

The Challenge

A standard FMCG product (mustard oil) needed a visual overhaul to compete in the premium organic market. Standard product photography felt too generic and lacked impact.

The Approach

I engineered a high-impact commercial photography concept utilizing 8K resolution, hyper-realistic rendering, and perfect symmetry. The goal was to elevate a common household item into a luxury branding aesthetic.

The Impact

The new visual direction completely altered consumer perception, allowing the brand to successfully launch their premium tier with visuals that justified the higher price point.

The Approach

I conceptualized an editorial photoshoot style that blended contemporary commercial fashion with subtle cultural motifs, ensuring realistic placement and grounding of footwear within complex layout environments.

Aura Aesthetics

Lead Visual Designer

The Challenge

The client's previous seasonal promotions were visually cluttered, diluting the perceived value of their high-end jewelry pieces and distracting from the product craftsmanship.

The Approach

I stripped back the visual noise, opting for an ultra-realistic, minimalist mockup environment. By focusing on premium lighting and removing distracting elements, the product became the sole hero.

The Impact

The refined editorial aesthetic established stronger premium positioning, directly resulting in a significant uplift in engagement across their digital storefront during the holiday season.

Valentine's Engagement Campaign

Campaign Designer

The Challenge

A premium luggage brand needed to participate in Valentine's week without resorting to cliché visual tropes that would cheapen their high-end reputation.

The Approach

I created a static social media concept that merged the emotional resonance of the holiday with the durability of the product, using sophisticated lighting and subtle typography.

The Impact

The creative maintained the brand's luxury positioning while participating in a trending conversation, leading to high organic shareability.
